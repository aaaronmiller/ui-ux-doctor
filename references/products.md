# Product Type to Style Mapping

| Product | Recommended Styles | Color Direction | Typography |
|---------|-------------------|-----------------|------------|
| Project Management | Swiss Grid, Corporate Clean | Blue primary, green accents | Sans-serif, clear hierarchy |
| CRM | Data-Dense, Corporate Clean | Blue/teal, trust palette | Compact sans, number-friendly |
| Analytics Dashboard | Data-Dense, Muted Dark | Dark bg, bright data colors | Monospace for numbers, sans for labels |
| Email Platform | Corporate Clean, Minimalist | Neutral with brand accent | System-like, readable at small sizes |
| Collaboration Tool | Scandinavian, Bento Grid | Warm neutrals, colorful avatars | Friendly sans, rounded |
| HR Platform | Corporate Clean, Scandinavian | Warm blue, green success | Accessible, generous spacing |
| Accounting | Data-Dense, Government | Conservative blue/gray | Tabular numbers, clear alignment |
| Help Desk | Corporate Clean, Swiss Grid | Blue primary, status colors | Clear hierarchy, scannable |
